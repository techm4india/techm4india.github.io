Academic Credit Programs

Three Programs. Seven Credits. One Career Transformed.

PDP, RBLP, and LSEP together generate 7 UGC-format academic credits per student — directly fulfilling NEP 2020 Criterion 2 and 3 of NAAC. Built for engineering colleges that want to matter.

📊
PDP · 2 Credits

Professional Development Program

A structured 2-credit course covering industry communication, project management, technical presentation, and professional ethics. Designed in collaboration with HCL and Tech Mahindra HR teams to match what companies actually look for in freshers.

RBLP · 5 Credits

Research-Based Learning Program

A 5-credit research course where students work in guided teams on real deep-tech problems sourced from our MoU partners. Teams are mentored by IIT faculty and industry leads. Every RBLP project is submitted for potential incubation at T-Hub.

LSEP · Internship

Live Software Engineering Program

A 6-month internship where students work directly on TechM4Solutions' deployed AI and SaaS products — EAMCET Predictor, AutoHire.AI, RythuSeva, SchoolOS. Not a simulation. Students maintain real code in production with real users. Assessment rubric provided to institutions.
