Our Origin Story

Started With a Question:
"Why Can't Every Student Access World-Class Tech Education?"

TechM4India was born from frustration — watching brilliant minds in tier-2 and tier-3 cities of Telangana graduate without real skills, without confidence, without a roadmap.

Our founder's vision was bold: Build not just a coaching centre, but an entire technology ecosystem — one that covers engineering colleges, innovative product solutions, aerospace education, and school-level transformation simultaneously.

Today, that vision is a reality. With 4 powerhouse divisions operating across India, TechM4India is proving that geography is no longer a barrier to excellence.

Our Journey

From First Idea to National Impact

Every milestone is a story of students who believed, mentors who showed up, and a team that refused to settle.

2022 — The Spark

TechM4India is Founded

Founded with a clear mission: bridge the skill gap between what colleges teach and what industry demands. First batch: 45 engineering students from Telangana.

2022
2022
2022 — First Products

Solutions Division Launches

Our team ships EAMCET College Predictor and StaySecure — real products, real users. Proves students can build production-grade software from day one.

2023 — Sky's No Limit

Space Technology Division Born

India's first school-level space lab curriculum launches. Students build and launch model rockets, program satellites, and study orbital mechanics.

TechM4India Schools Division

CareerBuddy AI and SchoolOS platforms launched. 50 schools in first cohort. 3,000 young minds ignited with the power of technology and self-belief.

200+ Institutions. 10,000+ Students.

TechM4India signs partnerships with engineering colleges, CBSE schools, and state government skill development boards. The movement goes national.
