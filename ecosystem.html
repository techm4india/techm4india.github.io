🏫 TechM4Schools

Building the Pipeline Before Students Reach College

India's education problem starts before engineering college. When students arrive at Year 1 without any exposure to real technology, computational thinking, or scientific curiosity — no college curriculum can fully bridge that gap. TechM4Schools fixes this at the source.

Mahi has personally visited over 500 schools across 7 Telangana districts — not from a spreadsheet. We understand the ground reality: most schools have zero lab infrastructure, teachers who want to teach but lack tools, and students whose potential is hidden behind circumstance.

Our answer: five lab formats designed for India's school infrastructure reality, curriculum mapped to NEP 2020 and state board requirements, teacher training built in, and a connection to TechM4Engineering so the best students graduate into a world they've already started exploring.

⚙️ TechM4Engineering

Six Labs. Three Programs. One Transformed Institution.

TechM4Engineering is the flagship university vertical. We don't just install labs and leave. We bring the full stack: six deep-tech research labs, three credit-bearing academic programs, industry mentors from HCL, JPMorgan, Deloitte, and Tech Mahindra, and an outcome measurement framework that feeds directly into NAAC accreditation.

The three programs — PDP (2 credits), RBLP (5 credits), and LSEP (6-month live internship) — together generate 7 UGC-format academic credits per student. Every requirement under NEP 2020 Criterion 2 and 3 of NAAC is directly fulfilled. Institutions don't need to choose between compliance and excellence. This delivers both.

💡 TechM4Solutions

Real Products. Real Clients. Student-Built.

TechM4Solutions is not a training company's side project. It's a functioning product and technology division that builds and deploys AI and SaaS tools for real clients — schools, colleges, businesses, and government bodies.

But here's what makes it different: the LSEP internship program places final-year engineering students directly on these product teams. Students aren't building mock projects — they're maintaining production code that real users depend on. Their work ships. Their bugs get fixed. Their features reach thousands of users. That portfolio is worth more than any certificate.

🚀 TechM4Space Technology

India's Most Ambitious EdTech Vertical. No Competition.

TechM4Space is something that simply doesn't exist anywhere else in Indian EdTech. We run active aerospace research and education programs — CanSat mission design, drone engineering, propulsion systems, and SpaceData analytics. Students don't study aerospace. They do aerospace.

India is going to need thousands of aerospace engineers in the next decade. ISRO, private space companies, drone manufacturing, and satellite communications are all growing faster than colleges can produce graduates. TechM4Space is building that pipeline — starting at the lab level in engineering colleges across Telangana.
